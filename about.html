15+ Years of Service

Built on Family Values

Dave's Removals & Sons was started by Dave over 15 years ago with nothing more than one van and a dream to build a removals company that truly cared about its customers. What began as a one-man operation has grown into a proud family business, with his sons now working alongside him every step of the way.

Over the years, we've expanded from that single van to a fleet of modern, well-maintained vehicles — but our core values have never changed. We pride ourselves on treating every customer's belongings as if they were our own, handling each item with the care and respect it deserves.

As a local business, we've grown almost entirely through word of mouth and repeat customers. There's no greater compliment than a customer recommending us to their friends and family, and that trust is something we work hard to earn on every single job.
