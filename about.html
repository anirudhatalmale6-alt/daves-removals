About Dave's Removals & Sons

A family-run business built on trust, hard work, and a genuine care for our customers.

15+ Years of Service

Your Belongings, Fully Protected

Dave's Removals & Sons was founded over 15 years ago with one simple promise: every item we move will be treated as if it were our own. What started as Dave and a single van has grown into a trusted family operation, with his sons now by his side on every job. Together, we've built a reputation for damage-free moves and complete customer satisfaction.

We are fully insured and carry comprehensive transit protection on every move we do. From the moment we pick up your first box to the moment the last piece of furniture is placed in your new home, your belongings are covered. Our team is professionally trained in safe lifting, secure wrapping, and careful handling of fragile, valuable, and oversized items. We use premium packing materials, purpose-built trolleys, and protective blankets to ensure nothing gets scratched, dented, or broken.

Our growing fleet of modern, well-maintained trucks is equipped with tie-down systems and air-ride suspension to keep your goods safe in transit. We conduct pre-move walkthroughs to plan the safest route for every item, and our team takes extra care with doorways, stairwells, and tight spaces. We don't rush — we do it right.

As one of the Shoalhaven's fastest-growing removals companies, we've earned our reputation through thousands of successful, damage-free moves. Our clients come back to us time and again, and refer us to their friends and family. That trust means everything to us, and we work hard to earn it on every single job.
